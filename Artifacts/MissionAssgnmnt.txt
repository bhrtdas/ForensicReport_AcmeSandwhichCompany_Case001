The Problem

Network sensors at Acme Sandwhich company detected a possible Windows shell being sent over TCP. The owner of Acme Sandwhich is concerned that his super secret BLT recipe was stolen. 

The computer that stores the recipe is not accessible from the external network. 

The Assignment

Identify if any of the systerms were compromised
Map the network topology
Determine if the secret sandwhich recipe was stolen
Make recommendations on security practices
